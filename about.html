01. Team Identity

Team Rotor FPV is a specialized drone development collective at VIT University, Vellore. We operate at the intersection of high-speed aerial cinematography and advanced robotic engineering.

Our work spans from designing ultra-light racing frames to developing autonomous flight algorithms for search and rescue simulations.

02. Workspace & Grounds

Operating out of Creation Labs, our hangar serves as a primary hub for UAS research. We maintain a full suite of rapid prototyping tools including CNC carbon cutters and precision soldering stations.

Our flight testing occurs at designated university grounds, providing the clearance necessary for high-Betaflight tuning and 6S power system stress tests.

04. Frequently Asked

How can I join Rotor FPV?

Recruitments happen once a year. We look for students passionate about embedded systems, structural design, or professional piloting.

Do I need to own a drone to join?

No. We provide the equipment, simulators, and training required to go from a beginner to a certified team pilot.
