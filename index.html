About Team RotorFPV

Team Rotor FPV is the first drone-specific team in VIT Vellore, formed in 2018 with the aim of developing a group of pilots aiming for national and international events. Composed of drone enthusiasts hustling together to fly high and beyond, the team has grown over the years to include 35 specialized pilots. We take pride in having nurtured the first female pilot of VIT and maintain a strong, ever-growing roster. Our focus remains dedicated to the cutting edge of aerial robotics, specializing in high-speed Racing drones, creative Freestyling, and complex Autonomous drone systems.
