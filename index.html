Team Overview

Engineering the
Skies of Tomorrow

Team Rotor FPV is a dedicated drone development team at VIT, Vellore, focused on building high-performance FPV drones and advancing aerial robotics. We specialize in drone design, assembly, and real-time flight testing.

We work out of GDN 129, where all our drones are designed and built from scratch. Flight testing is carried out at SJT Ground and KC Lawn, ensuring optimal performance under real-world conditions.
